Cat Facts

Cats are fascinating creatures! Here are some interesting facts about them:

  • Cats have been domesticated for over 4,000 years.
  • Cats have flexible bodies and can rotate their ears 180 degrees.
  • Cats have powerful night vision and can see at only one-sixth the light level required for human vision.
  • Cats have retractable claws, which they use to catch prey and defend themselves.
  • Cats have a strong sense of smell and can detect certain odors at concentrations as low as one part per billion.
